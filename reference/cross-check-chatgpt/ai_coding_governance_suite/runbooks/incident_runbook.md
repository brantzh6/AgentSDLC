# Incident Runbook

## Purpose
This runbook gives the control agent a compact method for handling runtime issues.

## Step 1 — detect
Record how the problem was discovered.

## Step 2 — triage
Decide whether to:
- stop
- disable
- roll back
- isolate
- continue with caution

## Step 3 — stabilize
Restore control first.
Do not continue expansion or rollout while the system is unstable.

## Step 4 — analyze
Record:
- trigger
- symptom
- impact
- direct cause
- deeper enabling condition

## Step 5 — fix
Separate:
- temporary mitigation
- permanent correction

## Step 6 — harden
Add at least one durable improvement:
- test
- monitoring
- checklist
- rule
- runbook
- design constraint

## Step 7 — close
Only close when:
- the system is stable
- the fix is in place
- anti-recurrence action is also in place
